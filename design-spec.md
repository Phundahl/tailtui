Interaction Patterns (Overlays) All secondary management tasks use centered modal overlays to maintain context without full-page transitions. ### Account Management (`[l]`) * **Interface**: Centered modal with a high-density account list. * **Keybindings**: * `[j/k]` Navigate accounts * `[Enter]` Switch to selected account * `[a]` Add new account * `[d]` Remove account (double-press to confirm) * `[q/Esc]` Close overlay ### Subnet Router Details (`[e]`) * **Interface**: Centered modal with a routing table display. * **Columns**: Destination, Gateway, Latency, Status. * **Status Logic**: Green (ROUTING), Yellow (DISABLED), Red (CONFLICT). ### Help & Shortcuts (`[?]`) * **Interface**: Centered modal organized by category (Navigation, Node Actions, Global). ## 3. Visual Identity & Styling * **Theme**: "Matrix Core" (Pure TUI Aesthetic). * **Color Palette**: * Background: `#0e150f` (Deep Green-Black) * Primary: `#4ade80` (Neon Green) * Selection: High-contrast green highlight block. * **Typography**: JetBrains Mono (Nerd Font support for icons). ## 4.
